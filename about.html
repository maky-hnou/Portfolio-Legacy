About Me

I started as a Network Engineering Student. Back to 2018, I had to do a graduation internship before I get my Masters Engineering diploma. I had many interviews with companies in different fields. However, I was more curious and eager to learn about Artificial Intellignce. So, I ended up doing my internship in Novel-Ti (a Tunisian startup) and discovering more about AI and Computer Vision.

In September 2018, I started working fulltime at Novel-Ti as an R&D Engineer. Here I use my technical knowledge to collaborate with my colleagues and help building AI and Computer Vision solutions that solve real world problems.

Any Questions?

Why did you make this website?
I created and deployed this website as a free time activity to learn more about web development.

How long have you been practicing machine learning?

I discovered machine learning during my Masters studies. Since then, I have committed to learning everything I could about AI and its sub-fields.

I have rich experience in building Machine learning solutions and deploying them into production environments. I have worked with most of the ML and DL libraries and frameworks like scikit-learn, TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch...

Do you have any experience in Computer Vision?

During the last two years, I have worked on several projects related to Computer Vision. I have solid knowledge of the different CV libraries like OpenCV, PIL, Scikit-image...

I also spend most of my free time working on side projects that are mainly about Computer Vision and NLP too and post them in my GitHub account.

Why are you interested in NLP / Data Science?

I am curious by nature. what intrigues me most about Data Science and Natural Language Processing is their growing role almost in every field. Therefore, I aspire to learn more about them and be part of the communities working to spread this knowledge.

Despite the hype around Data Science and AI, the implementation of solid machine learning (especially NLP) tools is still fledgling. This field is one of the most challenging ones, thus I want to work with the pioneers and innovators of these fields.

What kind of work do you see yourself doing?

I am interested in building modular machine learning applications that can communicate with existing production systems.

Position titles that do this work are not well defined. I describe my perfect future position to be a mix of data science, machine learning engineering, and devops. Essentially a machine learning specialist with the skills to deploy a model to production.
